About Us

Woof is a small organisation founded on 29th October 2018 by a duo who love dogs. Our wonderful and loving team work hard to provide a home-like environment by delivering a safe, welcoming and fun experience for all our dogs. We want to build a strong connection with our clients and give them a stress-free, simple and guilt-free experience by staying connected through social media.
